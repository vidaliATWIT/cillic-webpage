FAQ

I will occasionally receive emails asking about the site and what it's for. While I don't mind, I think it's fitting to provide some sort of clarification for the casual visitor.

    What's the Cillic Archive?

    I first made this page to catalog all the things I was doing one particularly productive summer in 2020. Since then it's become the de-facto portfolio for my work and non-work.

    What kinds of things do you post?

    Whatever I'm working on in the worlds of music, software, writing, and occasionally video.

    Who owns this page?

    Ian G. Vidal.

    How often does this page get updated?

    Previously almost never, but I've made improvements in the site's infrastructure that should remedy that in the future.

    Is this a blog?

    No. If any of the URLs for this page ever link to a Blogspot, Medium, or Substack, then I am certainly dead, and this page has been compromised by outside forces.
